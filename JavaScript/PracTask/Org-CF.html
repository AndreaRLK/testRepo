Dietary Services

If logged in as an Employee, they are authorized to have access to Dietary Services.
If logged in as an Enrolled Member, they are authorized to have access to Dietary Services and consulatation with a dietician.
If logged in as a Subscriber, they are authorized to have partial access to facilitate Dietary Services only.
If unable to login as an Employee, Enrolled Member or Subscriber, they need to enroll or at subscribe to obtain general services.
